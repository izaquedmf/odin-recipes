Chicken

This is the only crispy fried chicken recipe you'll ever need. Why? It combines all the tried-and-true tricks to ensure the juiciest, most flavorful, crispiest chicken every time.

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour for coating
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Take your cut up chicken pieces and skin them if you prefer.
  2. Put the flour in a large plastic bag (let the amount of chicken dictate the flour amount).
  3. Season the flour with paprika, salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Dip chicken in buttermilk, then coat in flour mixture.
  5. Place coated chicken on a cookie sheet/tray, cover with dish towel/waxed paper.
  6. Let sit until flour reaches paste-like consistency.
  7. Fill large skillet (cast iron) 1/3-1/2 full with vegetable oil.
  8. Heat oil until VERY hot.
  9. Brown chicken in HOT oil on both sides.
  10. Cook covered for 30 minutes, then uncover and fry until crispy.
  11. Drain on paper towels.
  12. Fry in shifts if necessary; keep finished chicken warm.
